Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Do I need to install anything?
No! LeadFlow is 100% cloud-based. Open your browser, sign up, and start. Works on any device — laptop, tablet, or phone.
How does WhatsApp integration work?
You scan a QR code once (like WhatsApp Web). After that, all sends happen automatically — invoices and quotes are auto-converted to PDF and sent via your WhatsApp account.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Your data is stored in MongoDB Atlas (enterprise-grade cloud database), protected with JWT authentication and AES-256 encryption. All data is isolated — only you can access your data.
Do you provide GST invoice for the subscription?
Yes! All subscription payments via Razorpay come with a GST-compliant invoice sent to your registered email.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel anytime and you'll retain access until the end of your billing period. We also offer a 7-day money-back guarantee.
How many users/leads can I add?
All plans include unlimited leads, clients, invoices, and quotes. There are no artificial limits on records.
Can I migrate from my current CRM?
Yes! LeadFlow includes a built-in data migration tool. Import leads, clients, and invoices from Excel/CSV or from JSON exports. Our support team can also help with migration.
